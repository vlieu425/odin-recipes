Air-Fried Chicken Nuggets

Description

How to Make 3-Ingredient Air-fried Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of cooked chicken breast
  • â…“ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • large egg
  • Hot sauce (optional)
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F. Drain any liquid from the canned chicken and add the chicken to a bowl. Shred finely using forks.
  2. Add the cheese, egg, and hot sauce (if desired) to the bowl and stir until thoroughly combined.
  3. Form 6-7 chicken patties from the mixture. Spray the nuggets and the air fryer basket with cooking spray. Cook for 15-20 minutes, and allow to cool before serving.
